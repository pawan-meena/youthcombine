Plans and strategies that will help the Athlete improve and grow.

Youth Combine USA® Inc. will provide young athletes from across the country with a fun, safe, inclusive experience to test their athletic abilities in a variety of disciplines that builds on the foundation of sportsmanship, hard work, friendship, and teamwork.

Youth Combine USA® will help develop the life skills that will stay with young athletes long after they “hang up their cleats”.

1. Compliance with Regulations

  • All participants must adhere to the Youth Combine USA regulations and guidelines provided by the organizers. This ensures a fair and enjoyable environment for everyone.

2. Respect and Sportsmanship

  • All participants uphold values like respect, fair play, and teamwork, promoting a positive and inclusive atmosphere for all involved.

3. Fair Play

Promote fair play and adhere to the rules of each activity. Cheating or engaging in any form of unfair practices is strictly against the spirit of Youth Combine USA

4. Safety First

  • Your safety is our top priority. Follow all safety guidelines and instructions provided by our dedicated event staff. Report any safety concerns promptly.

5. Registration and Eligibility

  • Confirm your proper registration and eligibility before participating in any activities. Provide accurate information during registration, and inform us of any changes promptly.

6. Equipment and Attire

  • Use the appropriate equipment and wear the specified attire for each activity. Ensure your equipment meets safety standards to guarantee a secure and enjoyable experience.

7. Timeliness

  • Arrive on time for scheduled activities, and follow the event timeline. Punctuality is key to maximizing your participation and enjoyment.

8. Code of Conduct

  • Adhere to a positive and inclusive code of conduct. Any disruptive or offensive behavior may result in consequences, including expulsion from Youth Combine USA.

9. Communication

  • Maintain clear and respectful communication. Utilize designated channels for inquiries or concerns to ensure efficient and effective assistance.

10. Photography and Media

  • Participants may be photographed or recorded during the event. By participating, you consent to the use of your likeness in Youth Combine USA promotional materials.

11. Drug and Alcohol Policy

  • The consumption of drugs or alcohol during the event is strictly prohibited. Participants under the influence may face immediate disqualification.

12. Prizes and Awards

  • Prizes and awards, if applicable, will be distributed based on performance, adherence to rules, and embodying the spirit of fair play. Decisions by the organizers are final.

13. Notes

Organizers reserve the right to amend or add rules as necessary for the smooth operation of Youth Combine USA.

Participants will be notified promptly of any changes.

Thank you for being a part of Youth Combine USA, and we look forward to an inspiring and enjoyable event!
