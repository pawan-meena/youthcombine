Your Athletic Performance Score (APS)

The youth sports scene can be overwhelming, with pressure to specialize or join expensive programs. This can cause stress, injury, and burnout, leading kids to abandon the sports they once loved.

Youth Combine® Mission

Youth Combine allows kids aged 7-15 to assess their athletic skills in a fun, safe environment while promoting sportsmanship, friendships, hard work, and teamwork. Participants are evaluated at interactive stations and receive individual stats and nationwide comparisons. This feedback helps them celebrate strengths, address areas for improvement, and reach their full potential.
learn more

The Solution

After participating in a Youth Combine event, athletes receive their physical testing data and APS (Athletic Performance Score). It is not sport-specific; instead, it evaluates a range of skills common to various sports. The Youth Combine platform then allows athletes to view their results and compare them nationally by age, gender, and sport.

It’s a chaotic jungle out there in youth sports as parents pursue the dream of providing society with the next elite athlete. The journey is rough and costly in time, energy, and emotions.

A standardized, quantitative assessment that provides a clear picture of a young athlete’s overall skill set across multiple sports. , in some cases building a roadmap towards professional or NCAA decisions.

Athletes engage in Youth Combine® event (up to 2 X per year) Each event handles 500 young athletes ages between 7 and 15 years (in age specific cohorts) Youth Combine generate vast amounts of valuable sports science data

  • Once combine is completed, the athlete will have a current APS specific to their age category and independent of any one sport
  • The individual APS reflects an athlete’s true and quantitative abilities along with their growth patterns
  • The Youth Combine® smart /social app offers vast visualization tools that make the easy sharing of information to friends, family, coaches, and colleges
  • Youth Combine® participation and year-over-year APS data allows for rational planning and decision making as the coach, parent and young athlete consider (pursue) navigate specific sport decisions and/or NCAA scholarships

Micah Parsons, NFL Linebacker

Micah Parsons is an American standout NFL linebacker for the Dallas Cowboys and is one of the NFL’s most formidable defensive players. In the 2021 NFL Draft, Parsons was selected 12th overall. He earned NFL Defensive Rookie of the Year honors in his rookie season and is a 2x Pro Bowl selection. Known for his incredible speed, versatility, and football intelligence, Micah continues to be a game-changer for the Cowboys, consistently delivering impactful plays and solidifying his reputation as a top-tier linebacker.

Off the field, Micah is dedicated to giving back to the community and inspiring young athletes. His involvement with Youth Combine USA showcases his commitment to empowering the next generation through sports and education.
