Plans and strategies that will help the Athlete improve and grow.

Youth Combine USA® Inc. will provide young athletes from across the country with a fun, safe, inclusive experience to test their athletic abilities in a variety of disciplines that builds on the foundation of sportsmanship, hard work, friendship, and teamwork.

Youth Combine USA® will help develop the life skills that will stay with young athletes long after they “hang up their cleats”.

1. When should I arrive for the Youth Combine the day of?

It is recommended that athletes arrive at least 30 minutes in advance of the start time of the combine and to finish your athlete profile registration. This will allow for a proper warm-up and familiarity of the site.

2. Will there be merchandise available on site for purchase?

Youth Combine USA and their brand partners may have merchandise available on site for purchase.

3. Will there be food and drink available during the Youth Combine?

Water will be provided to all participants at no charge on site. Restaurant partners of Youth Combine may also have food/drink available on site for purchase.

4. What type of shoes should I wear?

All athletes must wear running shoes equipped for use on artificial turf. No track or baseball metal spikes will be permitted on any surfaces.

5. Will there be photographers or videographers at the Youth Combines?

Yes, photos and videos will be taken at each Youth Combine. Check out our social media, you may be featured in some of our posts!

6. Will attending the Youth Combine potentially help me get recruited?

Yes, attending the Youth Combine can significantly enhance your chances of getting recruited. The event provides a unique platform for athletes to showcase their talents through various athletic stations, leading to the calculation of an Athletic Performance Score (APS). This comprehensive metric serves as a valuable tool for recruiters, colleges, and coaches to assess your overall athletic abilities. By participating in the combine, you not only demonstrate your skills but also gain exposure to potential opportunities for scholarships and collegiate sports programs. Networking with recruiters and engaging in the recruitment-focused aspects of the event can further solidify your presence in the athletic community and increase your visibility to those involved in talent scouting. Overall, Youth Combine offers a dynamic environment conducive to attracting attention and creating pathways for future athletic opportunities.

7. What is the best way to communicate with the Youth Combine to get my questions answered?

Your first step will be to review the FAQs to see if you have answered your question. If you still require assistance, please email [email protected]

8. How do I get my combine results? How long does it take?

You will receive an email within 48-72 hours after the combine session concludes.

9. How can I volunteer at a Youth Combine?

Yes! There are many opportunities to get involved with a Youth Combine in your city. Drop us a line at [email protected] to get more information.

10. What should I bring to the Youth Combine?

Athletes should come equipped with running shoes, shorts and your official Youth Combine t-shirt. We encourage you to leave any valuables at home. We are not responsible for any stolen or misplaced items.

11. Can I register a private session for our association?

Yes. However, available dates are limited in 2024. For more information on private association combines for multiple teams please email [email protected]

12. How do I get to the venue?

See the events page for the location of each venue.

13. How will I know if I have been registered to the Youth Combine?

Upon completion of your online registration you will receive an email to complete your registration form and electronically sign the Waiver and Release Form. In addition you will receive an additional email with additional details for your upcoming Youth Combine. If you feel you have registered but did not receive an email please contact us at [email protected] and include the name you registered under along with the combine location.

14. Can parents, relatives or friends attend the Youth Combine as spectators?

Yes! Parents, relatives and friends are welcome to attend the Youth Combine as a spectator and enjoy the experience. Each registrant is provided with one credential allowing a parent/guardian onto the field throughout the combine. Unfortunately additional credentials for field level access are not available.

15. Do I get my money back if I cancel?

100% refunds are provided within 2 weeks of the Youth Combine. Cancellations within the 2 week window of your Youth Combine will have a $25 administrative fee charged.
